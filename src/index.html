Game Rules & Mechanics:

Objective: Stack the boxes perfectly to progress to the next level.

Stacking Rules:
Perfectly aligned stacks keep the box size consistent.
Misaligned stacks will shrink the box size based on the degree of misalignment.
Game Over Conditions:
The game ends if the box becomes too small to continue.
Missing a stack entirely also ends the game.
Winning Conditions:
Successfully stacking all required boxes for the level allows you to progress to the next destination.
Reaching Mars signifies the completion of the current game challenge.

ABOVE AND BEYOND

Welcome to Zyck's Adventure!

Meet Zyck, an alien from the well-known red planet, Mars.
His spaceship crashed on Earth due to low fuel while traveling across the universe.
To return home, Zyck must rise above Earth by stacking boxes higher and higher.
Can you help Zyck reach his home planet and continue his intergalactic journey?
